History of Herbs and Spices

Scientific Evidence shows that culinary herbs and spices may have beneficial effects in areas such as heart health, cognition, and weight management. Supporting the wisdom of our ancestors.

Writings of Charaka and Sushruta II (1st and 2nd century) referenced spices and herbs such as cinnamon, cardamom, ginger, turmeric, black pepper, cumin and mustard seed.
